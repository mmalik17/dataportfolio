Analyze E-Commerce Customer Purchases
Based on Job and Region

Purchasing activity of the customer is affected by several factor, such as economy or the region. In this dataset, I analyze the relationship between the purchase price variable to job and address variable. The job data is related to economic factor while the address data is related with the region factor. The purpose of this dataset is giving the insight to the company about which region or occupation that contribute the highest sales.

Objectives
1. Identify which state or city that give the highest revenue by looking at the highest purchase price column
2. Identify which occupation that give the highest revenue by looking at the highest purchase price column

Challenge:
There is no column named 'region' in the dataset. The region data should be extracted from 'Full Address' column. Hence, the full address column need to be separated into several parth, such as City, State, and Postcode

Identifying Spam Email Text with
Natural Language Processing

Spam email is an unwanted message that come to our email account, mostly from mass broadcast. Usually, spam messages have certain simillar pattern, such as offering discount, offering free product, etc. The opposite of the spam email is ham email.

In this project, the machine learning model predicts which email considered as spam or ham. The data is splitted into two section, 80% training data and 20% test data. The future purpose of this machine learning is selected the spam email to spam folder, so the primary folder is cleaned from spam or unwanted email
Objectives:
Create a model that could differentiate spam and ham email with natural language processing (NLP) technique

Challenge:
The model should have accuracy more than 85%
